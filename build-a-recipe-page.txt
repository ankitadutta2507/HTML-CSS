    <h1>Mini Margherita Pizzas</h1>
    <p>Welcome to the ultimate guide for making mini Margherita pizzas! These cute and tasty personal-sized pizzas are perfect for a quick snack, party appetizer, or mini meal. With a crispy base, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey mozzarella, they’re a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Follow this simple recipe to enjoy delicious homemade pizzas right from your oven.</p>
    <img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/a4/Pizza.jpg" alt="Pizza" width="200"/>
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>1 cup all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>1/2 teaspoon instant yeast</li>
      <li>1/4 teaspoon salt</li>
      <li>1/3 cup warm water</li>
      <li>1 tablespoon olive oil</li>
      <li>1/3 cup pizza sauce</li>
      <li>1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese</li>
      <li>6–8 fresh basil leaves</li>
      <li>Optional: cherry tomatoes, chili flakes</li>
    </ul>
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.</li>
      <li>In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, and salt. Add warm water and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.</li>
      <li>Knead for 3–4 minutes until smooth. Let rest for 15–20 minutes.</li>
      <li>Divide dough into small balls and flatten into mini pizza rounds.</li>
      <li>Spread a spoonful of pizza sauce over each round.</li>
      <li>Top with shredded mozzarella and a basil leaf or two.</li>
      <li>Bake for 10–12 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden.</li>
      <li>Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your mini pizzas!</li>
    </ol>
